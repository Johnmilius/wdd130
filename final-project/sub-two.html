Why Choose My Lessons?

  • Personalized Instruction: Each lesson is tailored to fit your unique learning style and musical interests. We'll focus on the techniques and songs that inspire you.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Life can be busy, but that shouldn't stop you from pursuing your passion. Choose a schedule that works best for you, with lessons available once a week or every other week.
  • Fun and Engaging: Learning guitar should be enjoyable! My teaching style is relaxed and friendly, ensuring you have a great time while making progress.

Lesson Rates

  • $25 for a 30-minute session: Perfect for a quick, focused lesson that fits into your busy schedule.
  • $35 for a 45-minute session: Ideal for those who want a bit more time to dive deeper into learning.

Flexibility

  • Once a Week: Consistent practice leads to steady improvement. Keep the momentum going with weekly lessons.
  • Every Other Week: Great for those with a busy lifestyle or who need a bit more time between lessons to practice.
